About the Product

MyGuava Business is a powerful analytics dashboard designed for merchants to manage and monitor their payment operations across both physical POS terminals and connected e-commerce platforms.

As the product designer, I was responsible for designing the full dashboard experience from the ground up — including user flows, interface layout, data visualization, and usability logic. The main goal was to create a clean, intuitive interface that simplifies access to complex financial data.

Overview

Main dashboard with key metrics like total sales, average transaction size, and payment breakdown. Designed for quick insight at a glance using modular charts and KPIs.

POS/E-commerce

Displays active and inactive POS terminals and e-commerce merchants. Allows quick monitoring of each channel’s activity and health.

Charts

Interactive graphs showing trends over time — filtered by date, channel, and currency. Helps users compare performance across POS and e-commerce.

Applications

Tracks the status of submitted applications (e.g. onboarding, product access). Users can filter, view details, and take action in a side panel.
