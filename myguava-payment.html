MyGuava Payment

A customizable payment page solution designed for businesses to seamlessly integrate payment processing into their websites. The system allows merchants to create branded checkout experiences with flexible styling options, multiple payment methods, and secure transaction handling.

As the lead designer, I focused on creating an intuitive interface that balances customization capabilities with ease of use, ensuring businesses can quickly set up professional payment pages while maintaining their brand identity.

Checkout Screens

The checkout screens showcase the payment flow across different platforms and methods. The design is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices, ensuring a seamless experience for users on any screen size. All UI elements adjust fluidly to smaller viewports, with touch-friendly controls and simplified layouts.

Customizable Checkout

The checkout experience is fully customizable, allowing businesses to match their brand colors, fonts, and styling. Merchants can preview changes in real-time and ensure their payment page reflects their brand identity while maintaining optimal conversion rates.

Multiple Payment Methods

The payment page supports various payment options including credit cards, digital wallets, and mobile payments. Users can choose their preferred method with clear visual indicators and secure processing for each option.

One-Click Payment

Streamlined payment flow with one-click options for returning customers. The system securely stores payment information and provides quick checkout for improved user experience and higher conversion rates.

Digital Wallet Integration

Seamless integration with popular digital wallets including Google Pay and Apple Pay. The interface provides clear visual cues for supported payment methods and ensures smooth transactions across different devices and platforms.

Secure Information Collection

The payment form collects necessary customer information with clear field labels and validation. The design emphasizes security and trust while keeping the form simple and user-friendly to minimize cart abandonment.
